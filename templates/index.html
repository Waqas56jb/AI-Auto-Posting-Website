Frequently Asked Questions

            

                

                    How does the AI video creation work?
                    
                

                

                    
Our AI analyzes your text to understand the narrative structure, key points, and emotional tone. It then selects appropriate visuals, animations, and music to create a compelling video. You can customize every aspect before finalizing.

                

            

            

                

                    What video formats are supported?
                    
                

                

                    
We support all major formats including MP4, MOV, and AVI. The videos are optimized for each platform (9:16 for TikTok/Reels, 16:9 for YouTube, and 1:1 for Instagram posts).

                

            

            

                

                    Can I use my own voice or music?
                    
                

                

                    
Absolutely! You can upload your own audio files or record directly in the app. Our AI will sync the visuals perfectly with your audio.

                

            

            

                

                    Is there a free trial available?
                    
                

                

                    
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial with access to all Pro features. No credit card required to start.
