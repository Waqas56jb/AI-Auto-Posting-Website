How It Works

From idea to published content in just four simple steps - all powered by AI

1

Input Your Content

Provide voice notes, text inputs, or even rough ideas to our platform. Our AI understands natural language and can extract key messages, brand guidelines, and campaign objectives from your input. You can specify target audience, tone, and any specific requirements.

2

AI Content Generation

Our AI models create tailored content optimized for each platform. For TikTok: short, punchy scripts with trending hashtags. For LinkedIn: professional thought leadership pieces. For YouTube: detailed video outlines with SEO-optimized titles and descriptions. All while maintaining your unique brand voice.

3

Automate Video Editing

The platform automatically edits your raw footage or selects from your media library. It adds captions, transitions, branded intros/outros, and even suggests background music. For static posts, it creates visually appealing layouts with optimal text-to-image ratios for each platform.

4

Schedule and Publish

Our AI suggests optimal posting times based on your audience's activity patterns. You can review, make final tweaks, and approve content for immediate posting or schedule it for later. The platform handles all the publishing across your connected accounts automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI social media automation platform

How does the AI generate content?

Our platform uses advanced AI models like Claude, Gemini, and Llama to create tailored content based on your inputs, brand guidelines, and platform requirements. The AI analyzes:

  • Your past content performance
  • Industry trends and benchmarks
  • Platform-specific best practices
  • Your target audience demographics
  • Current social media trends and hashtags

You can provide feedback on generated content, and the system learns your preferences over time for increasingly accurate results.

Is my brand data secure?

Yes, we take data security extremely seriously. Our platform features:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transfers
  • SOC 2 Type II compliant infrastructure
  • Regular third-party security audits
  • Granular user permissions and access controls
  • Option for on-premise deployment for enterprise clients

We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent, and you retain full ownership of all content created through our platform.

Can I manage multiple brands?

Absolutely. Our platform allows you to:

  • Create separate brand profiles with unique voice and style guidelines
  • Manage multiple social accounts per brand
  • Duplicate successful content strategies across brands
  • Compare performance metrics across all managed brands
  • Set different access levels for team members per brand

Enterprise plans support unlimited brands with advanced collaboration features for agencies and large teams.

How often are platform integrations updated?

We continuously monitor and update our platform integrations:

  • Real-time updates when platforms change their APIs
  • Monthly feature enhancements based on platform updates
  • Quarterly complete reviews of all integration points
  • 24/7 monitoring for any service disruptions

Our team stays ahead of platform changes through official partner programs with TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, and YouTube to ensure uninterrupted service.

What kind of content performs best with your AI?

Our AI excels with various content types:

  • Educational content: How-tos, tutorials, explainers
  • Behind-the-scenes: Company culture, product development
  • User-generated content: Customer stories, testimonials
  • Trending topics: Newsjacking, viral challenges
  • Evergreen content: Tips, lists, best practices

The system provides performance predictions for each content type based on your specific audience data.

How does the automated video editing work?

Our video automation includes:
